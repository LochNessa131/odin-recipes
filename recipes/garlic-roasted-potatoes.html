Garlic Roasted Potatoes

Best basic recipe for making potatoes

This is a super easy recipe that will make any dish more flavorful and delicious! It is extremely easy to make and it is a blast to eat them. This is a recipe that the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds small red or white potatoes
  • 1/4 cup good olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic (6 cloves)
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Cut the potatoes in half or quarters and place in a bowl with the olive oil, salt, pepper, and garlic. Toss them until the potatoes are well coated.
  3. Transfer the potatoes to a sheet pan and spread out into 1 layer.
  5. Roast in the oven for 45 mins up to 1 hour (or until brown and crisp). Flip twice with a spatula during cooking to ensure browning
  6. Remove potatoes from the oven, toss with parsley, season to taste, and serve hot
