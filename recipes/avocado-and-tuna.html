Avocado and Tuna

This tuna stuffed avocado is a light, healthy dish that goes best with crisp white wines and crunchy bread. This recipe is great for experimenting with a variety of different vegetables, spices, and kinds of vinegar.

Ingredients

  • 1(12 ounce) can of solid white tuna packed in water, drained
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced, additional for garnish
  • 1/2 red pepper, chopped
  • 1 dash of balsamic vinegar
  • black pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch of garlic salte, (or made to taste)
  • 2 ripe avocados, halved and pitted

Steps

  1. Stir together tuna, mayonnaise, green onions, red pepper, and balsamic vinegar in a bowl.
  2. Season with pepper and garlic salt, then pack the avocado halves with the tuna mixture.
  3. Garnish with reserved green onions and a dash of black pepper before serving.
